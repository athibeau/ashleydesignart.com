Mobile Game Logo Farm Crisis: Idle Climate

Farm Crisis: Idle Climate is a mobile idle game set in a futuristic world where the earth’s surface is unlivable due to climate change. Farming underground becomes the only means of survival. Players must strategically manage their farms, workers, and crops in order to earn money and “climb out of the hole society put you in.”
The logo includes a leaf icon that will be used in strategic areas throughout the game. The leaf is meant to represent Agro-Eco Inc., a fictional company in the game that supplies players with “investments” in the form of in-game cash and loot boxes filled with boosts to increase profits.

Date: July 2019
Client: Bump Studios
