Personal Branding Steven Beshensky

Steven Beshensky is a web developer on a quest to make the web a better place to be. He asked me to assist with the design of his personal website sbesh.com, including the creation of a logo, typeface pairings, and overall design sense to make the site feel cohesive. The site design is meant to evoke a sense of unease or instability; it makes you second guess what youâ€™re looking at. The web is a very boxy place, and by creating a design strictly counter to that idiom, we were able to give birth to a unique design sense. The logo, made up of his initials, is bold and angular. I chose the typeface Trailmade for headings paired with Exo for body text. Trailmade feels very active and confident while Exo conveys a sophisticated, futuristic, technological feeling; all words I would use to describe Steven.
