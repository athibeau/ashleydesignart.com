Book Jacket 24-Hour Bookstore

This project focused on the use and relationships of text. I redesigned a book jacket by taking careful measurements to make sure the jacket fit the book well. I used serif typefaces in the layouts because they are reminiscent of the atmosphere of an old bookstore. The decision to use darker colors and an image of a partially eclipsed moon was inspired by the book's title. The typography on the front cover adds depth as the letters appear to follow the receding lines of the bookshelf.

Date: Fall 2016
Class: Graphic Design Studio II
