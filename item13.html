Logo Concept The Adventurous Co.

The Adventurous Co. is a clothing brand for outdoor and adventure enthusiasts. They sell merchandise themed around camping with intent to evolve into a camping gear company. I became connected with this company through the 99designs contest website. The company was looking for a minimalist, geometric logo design with a vintage style that incorporated their name. The first design I provided was a very simple design and earned me a spot as a finalist in the contest. I received feedback that the client really liked the typeface design I created, but would like to see a design that incorporated more of the outdoor theme. I submitted a new design with mountains that they liked, but in the end, the client chose another designer as the winner.

Date: June/July 2019
